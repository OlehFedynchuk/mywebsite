About "BUNKER"

The history of the company began in 2017, when two students of classmates decided to do tuning cars. For these purposes, the garage under number 25 was removed. Everything started with a simple set of alarms, sound insulation and auto sound. All the works were done with their own hands. Then gradually began to appear hired workers. In 2018, a corresponding LLC was registered, we left the garages and became one of the first automobile studios in Lviv, which professionally engaged in tuning and additional equipment of cars. Over time, thanks to the accumulated knowledge and experience, the focus of our work has shifted from simple installations to the segment of premium-class cars and integrated solutions. We began to work not only with retail clients, but also with most motor shows in Lviv. It was decided to move the "South" to the "Rick-Auto" car dealership, the only multi-branded salon in Lviv. This allowed us to be closer to one of our largest customers, as well as to maximize our working conditions. Due to the large volumes of work, in early 2018 it was decided to open another box. For today we offer our clients almost all kinds of tuning of the car. Having a huge experience of complex installations, we are ready to realize almost any, even the most non-standard desire of our client. Our employees are the best specialists in their field in Western Ukraine, and this ensures the maximum quality of our services.
